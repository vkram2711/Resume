# Bond Film Platform — Android Video App

**Role:** Android Developer
**Duration:** Jun 2018 – Oct 2018 (5 months)
**Location:** Kropyvnytskyi, Ukraine

---

## Overview

My first professional software job, taken at a young age. Bond Film Platform was a video publishing service similar to YouTube, and I was brought on to build their Android app from scratch.

---

## What I Built

Over 3 months I designed and shipped a full Android application covering the core user experience:

- **Video playback** — smooth in-app streaming with buffering indicators and full-screen mode
- **Video uploads** — background upload service with progress tracking
- **Search** — full-text search across the platform's video catalogue
- **User authentication** — registration, login, and session management
- **Notifications** — push notifications for new content and engagement events
- **Creator & viewer UX** — separate flows for content creators uploading videos and viewers browsing and watching

---

## Stack

- **Language:** Java
- **Platform:** Android (native)
- **Video streaming:** ExoPlayer
- **Backend communication:** REST API

---

## Takeaway

This was my introduction to professional Android development — I learned Java and mobile architecture on the job while shipping a real product. It established an early habit of diving into unfamiliar technology and delivering working software under deadline.
