## The Problem

News media is inherently biased. Different outlets cover the same event with different framing, selective facts, and editorial slant — and most readers only see one version. Over time, this creates filter bubbles and reinforces polarized worldviews.

## The Solution

The Gray Area is an AI-powered news aggregator that:

1. **Scrapes** the same story from multiple media outlets simultaneously
2. **Deduplicates and clusters** coverage by topic using semantic similarity
3. **Synthesizes** a balanced summary that presents multiple perspectives side by side
4. **Highlights** where coverage diverges — what one outlet emphasizes that another omits

The goal is not to tell readers what to think, but to show them the full picture so they can decide for themselves.

## My Role

I worked on the backend pipeline: news scraping, article clustering, and the summarization layer using a large language model. The team built the full prototype — from data ingestion to a working frontend — in under a week.
